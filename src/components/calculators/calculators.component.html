Advanced Mortgage Calculators

Balloon Calculator

The Balloon Calculator helps borrowers estimate their final balloon payment, which is a large lump sum due at the end of a loan term after smaller monthly payments have been made during the loan’s life. Balloon payments are common in certain types of loans, like commercial or short-term mortgages.

Breakeven Calculator

The Breakeven Calculator helps property investors determine the point at which a property’s income equals its expenses, meaning there is neither a profit nor a loss. This is useful to calculate the minimum occupancy rate or revenue needed to cover all expenses.

DSCR Calculator

The DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) Calculator helps lenders and investors assess whether a property’s income is sufficient to cover its debt payments. The ratio compares a property’s Net Operating Income (NOI) to its debt service (loan payments).

The Fix and Flip Profit Calculator helps real estate investors estimate potential profits by factoring in purchase price, rehab costs, and after-repair value (ARV). It reduces risk by identifying thin margins.
