Advanced Mortgage Calculators

Amortization Schedule

The Amortization Schedule Calculator builds a month-by-month table of payment, interest, principal, and ending balance. Enter loan amount, rate, term, and start date; the calculator outputs the fixed payment, total interest, total paid, and a downloadable schedule. Useful for comparing loan terms and showing payoff pacing.

Balloon Calculator

The Balloon Calculator estimates the lump-sum due at maturity for loans with smaller periodic payments. Provide loan amount, rate, term, and amortization basis; the calculator returns monthly payment during the term and the final balloon. Helps plan exits and refinance timing.

Breakeven Calculator

The Breakeven Calculator computes the occupancy or revenue level where effective income equals operating expenses. Enter rents and expenses; the calculator shows breakeven rent, units, and occupancy percent—ideal for underwriting minimum performance.

Bridge Loan Calculator

The Bridge Loan Calculator models short-term, interest-only loans with points, broker fees, reserves, exit fees, and other closing costs. It outputs monthly payment, total interest, net proceeds at close, payoff at exit, and an effective APR approximation.

Cap Rate Calculator

The Cap Rate Calculator computes capitalization rate from NOI and value, or solves for value from a target cap. Use the calculator to benchmark deals against market cap rates and to back into pricing from income.

Cash-on-Cash Return

The Cash-on-Cash Return Calculator measures annual before-tax cash flow divided by total cash invested. Enter income, vacancy, operating expenses, financing terms, points, reserves, and closing costs; the calculator outputs CoC %, DSCR, NOI, and cash invested.

DSCR Calculator
