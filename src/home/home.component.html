Lenders

Discover Pre-Screened Loan Opportunities

  • Browse real financing needs that match your lending criteria
  • Search by loan type, property type, loan amont, etc.
  • Contact originators directly — no gatekeepers, no wasted time
  • Grow your portfolio with high-quality commercial or residential deals
  • Build relationships with new clients

How It Works

  1. Loan Officers post a loan request (property type, loan amount, location, etc.)
  2. Loan Officers browse the lender directory to find new funding sources
  3. Lenders browse loan postings and send offers with terms
  4. Loan Officers review and choose the best option — it's that easy

Why It Works

  • Connecting originators and lenders directly
  • Faster than traditional lending
  • More competitive offers through lender competition
  • Built for transparency and efficiency
  • Avoid cold calling for business and loan placement
